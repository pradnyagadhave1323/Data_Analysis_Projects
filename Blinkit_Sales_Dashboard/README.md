# Blinkit Grocery Sales Analysis Dashboard
**Project Description**
The Blinkit Grocery Sales Dashboard provides a detailed analytical view of sales and outlet performance across multiple dimensions — outlet size, type, location, and product categories.
This interactive Excel dashboard helps decision-makers quickly analyze trends, compare outlet performance, and identify key growth opportunities.
The dashboard integrates visual charts, KPIs, and filters for real-time insights into total sales, average sales, number of items, and customer rating.

**Problem Statements**
Sales Overview:
Blinkit’s management needs a centralized dashboard to track overall grocery sales performance, average sales, and total number of items sold across outlets.
Outlet Size Analysis:
The company wants to identify how outlet size (Small, Medium, High) impacts total and average sales to optimize resource allocation and inventory management.
Outlet Location Performance:
There is a need to evaluate sales performance by outlet tier (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3) to understand which locations generate the most revenue.
Fat Content Insights:
Blinkit’s grocery sales include products with different fat contents (Low Fat, Regular). The company needs insights on how these categories perform in terms of sales and outlet distribution.
Item Type Analysis:
The management wants to identify top-performing item categories (Baking Goods, Breads, Breakfast, Canned, Dairy, etc.) to make better purchasing and promotional decisions.
Outlet Establishment Trends:
The company aims to study how outlet establishment year affects performance, tracking sales trends from 2011 to 2022.

**Key Features**
📊 Dynamic KPI Cards:
Displays Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items, and Average Rating.
🧩 Interactive Filters:
Filter data by Outlet Size, Item Type, and Outlet Location Type.
🧮 Category-Wise Insights:
Compare sales across item types (Dairy, Baking Goods, Canned, etc.) using bar and pie charts.
🏪 Outlet Performance Analysis:
Visualize sales by outlet size, outlet type, and outlet location tier.
📈 Yearly Trend Visualization:
Track sales performance over time through a dynamic line chart of outlet establishment years (2011–2022).
🥛 Fat Content Distribution:
Analyze the impact of Low Fat vs Regular items on sales and outlet distribution.

🎨 Modern UI Design:
Professionally designed Excel dashboard with clean layout, color-coded visuals, and icons for easy interpretation.
