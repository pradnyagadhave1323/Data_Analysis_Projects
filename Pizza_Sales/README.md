# Pizza Sales Dashboard – Excel and SQL Project
This dashboard transforms raw pizza sales data into clear, actionable business insights. It highlights revenue performance, daily/hourly sales patterns, category-wise contribution, top-selling items, and customer order behavior.

**Business Questions Answered by the Dashboard**

What is the total revenue, total orders, and total pizzas sold during the selected period?

Which day of the week records the highest order volume?

What are the peak sales hours during the day?

Which pizza category performs the best in terms of sales volume?

How are sales distributed across different pizza sizes (Small, Medium, Large, etc.)?

Which pizza items are the top 5 best sellers?

Which pizzas are the lowest-performing or least ordered?

What is the average order value and average pizzas per order?

How do sales vary daily and hourly across the selected time range?

How many pizzas were sold by each category (Chicken, Classic, Supreme, Veggie)?

How does the order trend change when filtering data by months, years, or a custom date range?

What insights can be used to plan promotions for peak days (e.g., Friday) and peak hours?
