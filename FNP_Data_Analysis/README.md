# FNP Data Analysis Dashboard
**Project Description**
The Sales Analysis of FNP (Ferns N Petals) dashboard is a data-driven Excel report designed to analyze sales performance across various occasions, products, and cities. It provides key business insights such as top-selling categories, customer spending behavior, and delivery efficiency.
This dashboard enables management to make informed decisions on inventory, marketing, and logistics by visualizing real-time patterns in sales and revenue.

**Problem Statements**
Occasion-Based Sales Insights – The company wants to understand which occasions (Diwali, Holi, Valentine’s Day, etc.) generate the highest revenue.
Category-Wise Revenue Analysis – Identify which product categories contribute most to the overall sales.
Monthly Revenue Tracking – There’s a need to compare sales trends across months to detect high and low-performing periods.
City-Wise Order Distribution – Determine which cities have the highest order volumes for better resource allocation and delivery management.
Product-Wise Performance – Analyze top-performing and least-performing products to optimize stock levels.
Delivery Time Monitoring – Evaluate average delivery duration to improve logistics and customer satisfaction.
Customer Spending Behavior – Find the average amount spent per customer to plan better pricing and promotional strategies.

**Key Features**
📊 Occasion Revenue Chart – Displays revenue generated from different festive and event-based occasions.
🛍️ Top 5 Categories Analysis – Shows the top-performing product categories like Cakes, Colors, and Mugs.
📈 Monthly Sales Trend – Tracks sales revenue month by month.
🧾 Product Revenue Report – Displays revenue generated from each individual product.
🏙️ Top 10 Cities by Orders – Identifies cities contributing the most to order volume.
💰 KPI Cards –
Total Revenue
Average Delivery Time
Average Customer Spending


🎨 Professional Dashboard Design – Minimalistic, color-coded (green theme), and easy to navigate.
