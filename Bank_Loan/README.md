# Bank Loan Dashboard
**Project Description**
The Bank Loan Report Dashboard is an advanced Excel-based analytical tool designed to monitor loan performance, assess credit risk, and analyze funding and repayment trends.
It provides a comprehensive overview of key loan metrics — such as funded amounts, received amounts, interest rates, DTI (Debt-to-Income ratio), and loan quality (Good vs Bad Loans).
This dashboard enables financial analysts and bank managers to make data-driven decisions by visualizing trends in loan applications, borrower characteristics, and loan purposes.

**Problem Statements**
Loan Portfolio Visibility – The bank lacked a consolidated view of total loan applications, funded amounts, and repayments across months.
Loan Quality Assessment – There was no efficient system to distinguish between good loans (fully paid) and bad loans (charged off).
Customer Segmentation Gaps – The management needed insights into how loans vary across employee lengths, states, home ownership, and loan purposes.
Performance Tracking – Decision-makers required month-wise and region-wise data visualization to identify high-performing areas and risk zones.
Interest Rate & DTI Monitoring – Needed a way to track the effect of average interest rate and DTI on loan performance over time.
Purpose-Based Loan Analysis – Required clarity on which purposes (medical, business, car, wedding, etc.) have the highest loan counts and default risks.

**Key Features**
📊 Summary Dashboard:
Total Loan Applications, Funded Amount, Amount Received
Average Interest Rate and DTI
Good Loan vs Bad Loan segmentation
Fully Paid, Charged Off, and Current loan breakdown
Visual comparison of loan performance metrics

🧾 Overview Dashboard:
Month-wise Loan Applications trend
State-wise Loan Application heatmap
Loan distribution by Employee Length, Purpose, Terms, and Home Ownership
KPI Cards showing MTD (Month-to-Date) and MTM (Month-to-Month) growth

Dynamic filtering by Grade and Loan Purpose
