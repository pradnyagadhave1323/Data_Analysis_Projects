**Project Description**
Vrinda Store Report 2025 is an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer demographics, and order insights across multiple e-commerce platforms. The dashboard helps business owners visualize trends, track top-performing regions, and make data-driven decisions.
It consolidates data from multiple sources and presents it in a clean, visual, and easily filterable format using pivot tables, slicers, and charts.

**Problem Statements**
Sales Performance Analysis – Lack of a centralized system to track monthly sales and order trends.
Order Status Monitoring – Need for a visual representation of delivered, cancelled, returned, and refunded orders.
Channel-Wise Sales Insights – Identify which online platforms (Amazon, Flipkart, Ajio, Meesho, etc.) generate the most revenue.
State-Wise Sales Performance – Determine the top 5 performing states to focus marketing efforts.
Gender-Based Insights – Understand purchase patterns between men and women.
Age Group Analysis – Analyze sales contribution across different age groups (Teenager, Adult, Senior).
Product Category Trends – Find out which categories (Blouse, Saree, Ethnic Dress, etc.) perform best.
Monthly Comparison – Track sales fluctuations across months to identify seasonal trends.

Key Features
📊 Order vs Sales Chart – Displays both total sales amount and total orders per month.
🥧 Order Status Pie Chart – Visualizes order distribution by delivery status.
🧾 Sales by Channel Report – Compares sales across different e-commerce platforms.
🏙️ Top 5 States Analysis – Highlights the most profitable regions.
👩‍🦰 Men vs Women Insights – Shows gender-wise order ratio.
👶 Orders According to Age – Analyzes customer base by age category.
🔍 Interactive Filters (Slicers) – Allows users to filter data by Month, Category, and Channel dynamically.
🎨 Clean Visual Design – Consistent color scheme, easy navigation, and structured layout.
